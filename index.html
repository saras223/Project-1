About us

About us

Valorcafe is a haven for passionate readers seeking solace and delight in a diverse array of coffees, teas, and delectable desserts. At Valorcafe, we're more than just a great spot to get coffee and desserts; we're a community of coffee lovers, book enthusiasts, and relaxation seekers.

Our adventure started with a straightforward concept: to provide a cosy and welcoming area where guests could enjoy the ideal cup of coffee and lose themselves in a good book.

Our story

Valorcafe was founded by a group of friends who shared a passion for the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the magic of a well-written book.

We envisioned a place where people might retreat from the busy world outside, a haven where life's little joys take front stage and time slows down.

Our coffee

We proudly sources its coffee beans from Colombia, our coffee offers a harmonious blend of flavours, and is delightfully sweet with hints of caramel and citrus. We are committed to ethical sourcing, supporting fair compensation for farmers and sustainable farming practices.

Savour the taste of Colombia in every cup and join us on a journey to the heart of South American coffee culture.

Visit us

The Valorcafe experience

We are located in the heart of San Francisco, our cozy cafe serves as the perfect retreat for those seeking a moment of respite or a gathering place for friends and coffee connoisseurs. Step through our doors and be greeted by the enchanting aroma of freshly brewed coffee.

Our cafe exudes warmth and charm, with comfortable seating, soft lighting, and the soft hum of conversation. Whether you seek solitude to lose yourself in a captivating book or a vibrant space to connect with fellow coffee enthusiasts, Valorcafe offers it all.

Plan your visit

Whether you're seeking a quick caffeine fix, a peaceful reading spot, or a venue to host your next event, Valorcafe is here to serve you. We are located at 7 Oakfield avenue, San Fransisco. We open 8am to 9pm on weekdays and 10am to 5pm on weekends.

Bookclub

Where stories come to life

On the first of each month, we explore a diverse selection of books, engage in deep discussions that dive into characters, plots, and the profound messages hidden within stories. It's a welcoming community where you can connect with fellow book enthusiasts, forge new friendships, and discover the magic of literature.

Join us at Valorcafe book club to share the joy of reading, explore new perspectives, and become part of a community that celebrates the enchanting world of books.

How to join

Participating in the Valorcafe Book Club is easy. Simply visit our cafe, inquire about our upcoming book club selections, and mark your calendar for our monthly meetings. There's no membership fee, and all are welcome to join.
